About Me

3D is my life, my passion, is the creation from nothing to everything

Hi :)!

My name is Mariona Miquel, and I've been working with 3D softwares since I was 16 years old. This is what my life is: modelling 3D project with SketchUp or 3Ds Max to create finished beautiful renders with V-Ray, post-process them with Photoshop, to finally create the layout for construction with AutoCAD.

If you want to see some projects, you're invited "to gossip" as much as you want through this web. Moreover, if you find some interest in a project, don't worry about bothering me and e-mail me. I will be enchanted to answer you.
